ABOUT US

ZAUCC is a multidisciplinary firm offering architecture, construction, interior design, and décor solutions under one roof. We specialize in delivering fully furnished residential and commercial spaces, managing every stage of the project from initial concept to final handover.
At ZAUCC, we blend creative design with technical precision to create spaces that are functional, refined, and timeless. Our services include architectural planning, structural execution, interior design, custom furniture, lighting, material selection, and complete décor styling—ensuring a seamless and stress-free experience for our clients.
ZAUCC doesn’t just build spaces—we design, construct, and furnish environments that are ready to live, work, and inspire.
